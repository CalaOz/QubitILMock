About Qubit

The Israeli Association for Quantum Technologies (Qubit) is dedicated to fostering the growth and success of Israel's quantum ecosystem. We bring together startups, academia, government, and investors to accelerate innovation.

Our mission is to position Israel as a global leader in quantum technologies, driving economic growth and technological superiority.
